Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Java Interview Quiz handles information when you use the game. The app is operated by an individual developer, David Paraschiv.

1. Contact

For privacy questions, you can contact the developer at: davidnicolap@gmail.com.

2. Quiz data and information we collect directly

The app does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

Java Interview Quiz presents Java interview questions from a question bank included with the app. You can select the first or second set of 100 questions. Questions and answer positions are randomized when a new game starts.

To remember an unfinished game, the app stores quiz progress locally on your device. This can include the selected question set, randomized question order, current question, score, coffee-cup progress, and current game screen. Restarting the game resets the score and coffee level. This information is used only to operate and resume the game and is not sent to the developer.

3. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show interstitial advertisements during gameplay, including after each group of 15 completed questions when another question remains. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google. AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide, measure, secure, and improve ads.

Depending on your device settings, region, and consent choices, AdMob may process information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID or similar identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • App interactions
  • Ad interactions
  • Diagnostics and performance information

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Consent and ad personalization

Where required, the app may ask for your consent for personalized advertising. You may also be able to limit or reset your advertising ID through your Android device settings.

5. Local storage

The app uses local storage on your device to save the quiz state described above. This data remains on your device unless you clear the app data, uninstall the app, or restart the game. The question bank itself is packaged with the app and is not built from your personal information.

6. Data sharing

The developer does not sell your personal data. Data processed by Google AdMob is handled according to Google's own policies and is used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and related purposes.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

8. Security

The app does not operate user accounts or store personal profile data on a developer-controlled server. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Summary

Java Interview Quiz does not require an account or ask you to submit personal details. It saves quiz and coffee-cup progress locally so an unfinished game can continue, and it uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements.
